# Assistant Surface Model

## Product surfaces
- docked assistant panel
- floating quick assistant
- proposal review view
- command explanation view
- apply/revert controls
- context pack preview
- job/progress monitor
- plan review surface for long tasks

## Surface law
The product assistant surfaces expose `L6A/L7A`.
They do not contain planning or authority logic of their own.
They must make scope, cost, proposal, and revert state visible.
