# StratumX Editor Product Constitution

The editor product exists to let a project be authored, simulated, debugged, packaged, and reviewed from inside one coherent Rust-native tool.
The editor product may be rich and broad.
It may never own lower-stack truth.

## Laws
- all mutation authority remains below the product layer
- all product surfaces are request/view surfaces
- suite breadth is encouraged; hidden truth duplication is forbidden
- closed views and inactive suites are cold
- the editor package remains universal and may not encode project-specific game taxonomy
