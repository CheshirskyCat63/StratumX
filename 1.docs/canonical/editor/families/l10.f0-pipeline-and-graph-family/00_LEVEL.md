# pipeline_and_graph_family Family

Canonical family: `pipeline_and_graph_family`.

## Role
This family groups related editor product surfaces or services that share data locality, activation regime, and request/view discipline.

## Owns
- bootstrap, import/export, graph, and package/dependency services

## Consumes
- service registries, diagnostics, package graphs

## Emits
- batch and graph service requests

## Family law
Members of this family cohere by data type, lifetime, and invalidation regime.
They may share indices, projections, and activation policy where legal.

## Never owns
- product or lower-stack truth
