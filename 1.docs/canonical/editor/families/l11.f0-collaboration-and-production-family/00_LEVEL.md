# collaboration_and_production_family Family

Canonical family: `collaboration_and_production_family`.

## Role
This family groups related editor product surfaces or services that share data locality, activation regime, and request/view discipline.

## Owns
- collaboration, review, gates, playtest/capture, dashboards, and onboarding surfaces

## Consumes
- collaboration metadata, diagnostics, build/release state

## Emits
- review/approval/playtest/report requests

## Family law
Members of this family cohere by data type, lifetime, and invalidation regime.
They may share indices, projections, and activation policy where legal.

## Never owns
- world or package truth
