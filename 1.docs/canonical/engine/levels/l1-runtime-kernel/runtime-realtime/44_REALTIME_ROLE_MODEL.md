# Realtime Role Model

## Role

Role contract for realtime runtime.

## Data Model

Realtime role may be local presentation only (`interactive-60`) or local presentation plus host-side remote burden (`listen-host-60`).
The role binds queue, visibility, network, replay, and simulation-tier entitlement ceilings at startup.
The role does not own transport, world truth, residency, or startup once bootstrap is complete.

## Role Matrix

| Profile | Mandatory ownership | Forbidden ownership | Failure posture |
|---|---|---|---|
| `interactive-60` | local presentation, bounded engine-owned runtime work | remote host burden beyond explicit local session law | refuse role widening |
| `listen-host-60` | local presentation plus bounded host-side remote burden | stealing local near entitlement for remote-only burden | degrade remote burden before local near entitlement |

## Canonical Law

- role widening after bootstrap is illegal;
- realtime role legality is derived from profile selection, startup wiring, and the integrated profile-composition proof;
- local presentable-frame ownership may not be traded away to hide network, replay, or visibility pressure;
- listen-host burden may not reverse degradation order or tier fairness.

## Illegal Patterns

- hidden host-role widening inside runtime or network services;
- spending local near-footprint entitlement on remote-only burden;
- using queued frames as a substitute for legal role composition.


## Role Matrix

| Role | Legal reserve | Illegal reserve |
|---|---|---|
| local interactive | local presentation reserve only | remote authority reserve |
| listen-host | local presentation reserve + bounded host reserve | unbounded remote burden on local near entitlement |
| observer-like runtime role | bounded presentation and audio only | authority over world progression |

## Rule

Realtime role binding is startup-owned and may not widen after bootstrap without restart.

## Role-Bind Matrix

| Bind concern | `interactive-60` | `listen-host-60` |
|---|---|---|
| local presentation ownership | mandatory | mandatory |
| remote host burden | forbidden except explicit local session law | explicit and bounded |
| startup bind freeze | mandatory before launch | mandatory before launch |

## Reserve-Seal Rule

`listen-host-60` may add remote burden only inside host-owned reserve.
It may not consume local near entitlement by silently widening role classes after bootstrap.

## Local Operating Law

Realtime role selection is not a soft mode bit.
It is a hard ownership contract that seals queue, visibility, network, replay, and simulation-tier entitlements before launch.
