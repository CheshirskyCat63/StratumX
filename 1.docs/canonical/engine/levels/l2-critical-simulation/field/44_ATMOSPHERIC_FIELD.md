## Role

Bounded atmospheric-field solve.

## Atmospheric Matrix

| Concern | Canonical law | Failure posture | Illegal posture |
|---|---|---|---|
| regional scope | bounded atmospheric regions only | approximate far regions first | planet-scale full solve every tick |
| wind/cloud/rain coupling | bounded field bridge records only | collapse low-value detail before breach | direct ownership of thermal/fluid truth |

## Rule

Atmospheric field is a critical simulation family surface, not a synthesis owner. It may publish bounded atmospheric outputs but may not own imaging queues, acoustics queues, or startup warm artifacts.

## Failure Posture

When atmospheric pressure rises, far-cloud detail, decorative turbulence, and long-range weather fidelity degrade before near-region legality or bridge ceilings are crossed.
