# Impact

## Role

Bounded impact-resolution surface.

## Impact Matrix

| Concern | Canonical law | Failure posture | Illegal posture |
|---|---|---|---|
| destructive impact class | bounded by impact tiers | downgrade decorative aftermath first | always-max impact solve |
| bridge emission | bounded same-tick records only | collapse excess records | impact becomes cross-family owner |
| replay/export shape | bounded shaped records only | drop optional aftermath detail | full raw impact dump every tick |

## Rule

Impact resolution may publish bounded aftermath records, but may not become a second owner of destruction, field, or material truth.

## Impact Class Matrix

| Impact class | Canonical posture | Illegal posture |
|---|---|---|
| gameplay-critical impact | preserve bounded legal resolution | hidden always-max aftermath |
| decorative aftermath | degrade first | persistent debris storm |
| cross-family emission | shaped and bounded | impact becoming foreign owner |

## Local Operating Law

Impact resolution may emit consequences, not ownership transfers.
Any local contract that lets impact own structural, field, or material truth is illegal.
